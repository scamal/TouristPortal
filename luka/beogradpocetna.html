Super dog Ricky

Elen, his boss

My dog Ricky has 30 years of age and has been living with me for the past 10 years. I found him out on the street, skinny and wounded. Even though I live in a flat I had to take him in. We have a lot of good moments together, one year we went on a holiday together in Hawaii. I put him along side my luggage in his doggy cage. He was calm during the flight trip. I have no kids but Ricky is an "object" that I give love to. I think adopting animals is an act of humanity and I respect all people who respect animals. My life with Ricky was very hard because he didn't want to eat any sort of food, until i came across "Dogs Food". I didn't believe but he ate only that specific type of food. I'm thrilled with "Dogs Food" and I'm using it to this day.

I'd recommend everyone food from "Dogs Food". Believe me, the dogs likes it. Many of my friends took my advice and did not regret it. I love "Dogs Food" and I wish you success!
